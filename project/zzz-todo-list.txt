application
-----------

display USA map
for each city
  put a circle on the map
  label the circle with city name

click on a city
  highlight the city (font size, bold, type color, circle color)
  show "link" to 4 closest neighbors
  highlight the 4 closest neighbors
  highlight the 4 peer cities by population size
  compute unemployment rate delta (2008 vs. 2017)
  produce bar chart
  produce small multiples time-series of unemployment rate


data
----

city data
- city name
- latitude
- longitude
- population
- list of 4 closest neighbors

population
- city
- population

unemployment rate
- city
- set of N years (2008 - 2017), "just July"
